Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view my grades?

To view your grades, log in to your account and navigate to the "Grades" section. There, you will find a list of your courses and the corresponding grades for each assignment or exam.

When are grades typically reported?

Grades are usually reported within one to two weeks after the completion of an assignment, exam, or academic period. However, the actual reporting time may vary depending on the specific course and instructor.

What should I do if I believe there is an error in my grade?

If you believe there is an error in your grade, you should first contact your instructor or teaching assistant to discuss the issue. They will be able to provide clarification and guidance on the next steps to resolve the matter.
