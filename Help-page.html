HELP AND SUPPORT PAGE

FAQs


How are grades reported?

Grades are reported on a semester basis and can be accessed through the student portal.


What is the grading scale?

The grading scale ranges from A (excellent) to F (fail).


How can I appeal a grade?

You can appeal a grade by contacting your professor or the academic affairs office.


How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.


Who can I contact for academic advising?

You can contact the academic advising office for academic advising.


What resources are available for tutoring?

There are various tutoring resources available, including peer tutoring and online tutoring services.


How can I access my course materials online?

You can access your course materials online through the student portal.


What do I do if I'm having technical issues with the online portal?

If you're having technical issues with the online portal, please contact our technical support team.
