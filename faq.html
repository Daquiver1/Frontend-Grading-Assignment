Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: How can I report a missing grade?
    A: You can report a missing grade using the "Missing Grade Form" on the dashboard page.
  • Q: How often are grades updated?
    A: Grades are typically updated at the end of each semester.
  • Q: How can I contact my instructor?
    A: You can find your instructor's contact details on the "Instructor Contact" page.
